# Family Reunion Portal — Future Ideas Bubble

This document acts as a creative roadmap for wholesome, non-addictive social features. The goal is to build connection and preserve family history without using predatory loops (like infinite scroll feeds, notification red-dots, or algorithms).

---

## 1. Clamped Profile Card Redesign

A non-scrolling screen card displaying a cousin's profile. Instead of a single vertical page, it is split into clean, swipeable slides/panels inside the card container.

### Slide A: Polaroid memory Carousel
*   **Media**: Supports a swipeable carousel of multiple profile photos.
*   **Short Clips**: Supports short, size-constrained looping video clips (up to 6 seconds, like an animated GIF or simple waving video) representing a warm visual hello.
*   **Decade Flashback**: A timeline slider (`[1990s] -- [2010s] -- [Today]`) that swaps the visual to show that person at different stages of life.

### Slide B: Life Milestones
*   **Concept**: A chronological list of major life events added by the user or family historians.
*   **Milestone Types**: Graduations (high school, college), Weddings, Births, Career Milestones, Retiring, etc.
*   **Details**: Each milestone has a Title, Date, Description, and can link to a specific photo in their Polaroid Carousel.

### Slide C: Auditory Heritage (Voice Notes)
*   **Concept**: A basic play/pause audio wave button.
*   **Usage**: Logged-in cousins can record or upload a short 10-second audio greeting (e.g. welcoming cousins, sharing reunion excitement).
*   **Historical Preservation**: Great for preserving the actual speaking voices of family elders.

### Slide D: Tribute Guestbook
*   **Concept**: A simple page where relatives can leave warm, static messages for each other.
*   **Behavior**: It is a plain, non-live text wall. You type a message, hit post, and it displays as a sticky note. No chat bubbles, typing indicators, or notifications—just a digital guestbook.

## 2. Homepage Family Bulletin (Broadcast Notifications)

A clean slide or panel on the homepage to share family news. It replaces the concept of an addictive social media timeline feed with a simple **Bulletin Board**.

*   **Broadcast Cards**: Family members can post a note to share upcoming family milestones or events:
    *   *"Maya has a soccer tournament in Dallas next Saturday! If you are in the area, come cheer her on!"*
    *   *"Marcus graduated! Celebration dinner at Olive Garden, June 20th."*
*   **Categorization**:
    *   `Youth Sports & Events`
    *   `Graduations & Achievements`
    *   `Announcements & Milestones`
*   **Basic Search/Filter**: Filter by category or branch. No comments or scrolling feeds; just cards that show the details and a contact email/phone if you want to reach out.
